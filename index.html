रमयते इति रामः

Welcome to our website,

Here, we present the sacred shlokas from Valmiki Ramayana with utmost reverence. the timeless wisdom of the Ramayana in all its glory, where every Kanda is meticulously presented with the original Sanskrit shlokas, their Kannada, English, Hindi translations, and more. At the heart of our platform is Shri Ram, the revered protagonist of this epic. His life stands as a shining example of virtue and righteousness, serving as an unwavering beacon of devotion to dharma (righteousness) and impeccable character. As the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu, Shri Ram's journey, intricately woven in the pages of the Ramayana, showcases his unyielding commitment to justice and truth. From his voluntary exile in the forest to his heroic battle against the formidable demon king Ravana to rescue his beloved wife Sita, Shri Ram's life serves as a timeless testament to duty, honor, and self-sacrifice. His profound teachings and exemplary actions continue to inspire and offer invaluable lessons on leadership, devotion, and the pursuit of a virtuous life.

Our vision extends beyond the Ramayana. In the future, we aspire to expand our platform to encompass the Mahabharata, Puranas, and more, offering a comprehensive repository of India's ancient wisdom. Join us on this enlightening voyage as we delve deeper into the vast ocean of India's timeless wisdom, guided by the luminous life and teachings of Shri Ram.

This project owes its inspiration to IIT Kanpur's Valmiki project.
